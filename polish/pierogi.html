Pierogi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup potatoes
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 onion (optional)
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Mix dough.
  2. Rest 30 min.
  3. Mix filling.
  4. Cut circles.
  5. Fill and seal.
  6. Boil 3-5 min.
  7. Fry (opt).
