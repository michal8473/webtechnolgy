Pierogi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 mashed potatoes (for the filling)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (for the filling)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional for the filling)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl, then add the egg and warm water. Knead into a smooth dough (about 5 minutes).
  2. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. For the filling, mix mashed potatoes, cheese, and onion (if using). Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut out circles using a glass.
  5. Add a spoonful of filling to each, fold in half, and seal the edges.
  6. Boil in salted water until they float (about 3-5 minutes), then drain.
  7. Optional: Fry in butter until golden and crispy.
