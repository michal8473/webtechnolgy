Krokiety z Grzybami Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour (crepes)
  • 1 cup milk (crepes)
  • 2 eggs (crepes)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (crepes)
  • 1 tbsp butter (crepes)
  • 1 lb mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter (filling)
  • Salt, pepper
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg (breading)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the crepes until golden.
  2. Prepare the filling.
  3. Stuff the crepes with the filling.
  4. Coat them in breadcrumbs.
  5. Pan-fry until crispy.
  6. Enjoy your delicious crepes!
